A long time ago, in a Galaxy far, far away… @shiltemann and @erasche were playing a traditional CTF event: hacking into systems, exploiting bugs, and learning about security. They had the crazy idea: «What about a Galaxy CTF?»

This is a competition (with no physical prizes) where you attempt to hack into a Galaxy instance and learn more about vulnerabilities, Galaxy Administration, and good security in the process.

Get Hacking!

About

When?

The CTF competition will start a week before GCC 2017, and run until the last night of GCC.

Who Can Compete

Anyone! We strongly recommend that you form teams involving at least one bioinformatician or biologist, and one computer person. Get out there, make new friends!

Rules of Engagement

  1. Please do not compete during presentations. There is plenty of time before the conference and during evenings in which to work on problems. We ask that you be respectful of the presenters.
  2. Only hack the Galaxy instance you have been given access to. Please do not exploit our infrastructure and ruin things for everyone.
